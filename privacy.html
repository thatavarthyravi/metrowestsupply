Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

At Metrowest Turnover Supply, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, delivery address, email, and phone number when you order a turnover kit.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to process orders, manage deliveries, and communicate regarding your account. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Protection

In compliance with Massachusetts 201 CMR 17.00, we maintain security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request a copy of your data or to ask for its deletion from our active records.
